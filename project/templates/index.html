Key Features

Immutable Certificates

Certificates stored on blockchain cannot be tampered with or forged.

Instant Verification

Verify certificates instantly using QR codes or certificate IDs.

Decentralized & Secure

No central authority needed, reducing fraud and increasing trust.

User Control

Recipients control who can access their certificates.

How It Works

For Issuers

  1. Register as an issuer
  2. Create and issue certificates to recipients
  3. Manage and revoke certificates if needed

For Recipients

  1. Register as a recipient
  2. Receive certificates from trusted issuers
  3. Share certificates with employers or institutions

For Verifiers

Anyone can verify a certificate's authenticity using the certificate ID or by scanning the QR code.
